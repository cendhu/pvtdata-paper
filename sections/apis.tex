\textbf{Walkthrough: Simulation and Blind Validation.}
$T_i$ executes: \texttt{GetState}($k_1$) $\to$ \texttt{PutState}($k_1$, $v_2$) $\to$ \texttt{GetPrivateData}($c_1$, $k_2$) $\to$ \texttt{GetPrivateData}($c_2$, $k_3$) $\to$ \texttt{PutPrivateData}($c_1$, $k_2$, $v_3$) $\to$ \texttt{PutPrivateData}($c_2$, $k_3$, $v_4$).
Before $T_i$ reaches validation, $T_j$ commits and updates $k_2$ in $c_1$ (version v5 $\to$ v8). The validator detects the stale read on $H(k_2)$ \emph{without accessing the private value}---blind validation.
